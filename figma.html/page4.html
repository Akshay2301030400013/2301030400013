PLEASE WORK ON IT FOR READING IMPROVEMENT
To improve your reading skills, focus on building a consistent and mindful reading habit. Start by choosing material that matches your current reading level but also challenges you slightly to expand your vocabulary and comprehension. Break the text into smaller sections and read actively, asking yourself questions about the main idea and details. Practice reading aloud to improve fluency and confidence, and don’t hesitate to reread difficult passages to gain clarity. Additionally, summarizing what you’ve read in your own words can strengthen retention and understanding. With time, patience, and regular practice, your reading skills will steadily improve.
