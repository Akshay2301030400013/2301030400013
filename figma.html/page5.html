The Two Friends and the Bear

Once upon a time, two friends were walking through a forest. They had promised to stick together no matter what. Suddenly, they saw a bear coming toward them. One friend quickly climbed a tree to save himself, leaving the other behind. The second friend couldn’t climb, so he lay down on the ground and held his breath, pretending to be dead.

The bear came close, sniffed him, and thought he was lifeless, so it walked away.

Once the bear was gone, the friend in the tree climbed down and asked, “What did the bear whisper to you?” The friend replied, “The bear told me to avoid friends who run away in times of trouble.”

Moral: True friends never abandon each other in difficult times.
